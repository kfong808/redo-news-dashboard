## Company

REDO is an ecommerce post-purchase platform serving DTC brands on Shopify, Shopify Plus, BigCommerce, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, and CommentSold. The product suite includes returns software (free for brands, funded by shopper protection fees), marketing cloud (email + SMS + Shopper-AI personas), abandoned-cart recovery via human + AI SMS agents (the Recover product, reaches the 95% of cart abandoners email/SMS can't legally touch), order tracking (Malomo, acquired), and AI customer concierge. Primary competitors are Klaviyo, Yotpo, Loop Returns, Attentive, Omnisend, Mailchimp, ShipStation, and Narvar. Mid-market sweet spot ($5M to $50M GMV brands).

---

## The Six Strategic Objectives

1. **Be findable when buyers search.** Win the moment a buyer goes looking for a marketing tool. Be present on every formal evaluation surface.
2. **Show up in moments of high intent.** Catch buyers when timing forces a decision (competitor pain, BFCM post-mortems, renewal-window calendars).
3. **Make it easy to close.** Decision-tools and partner channels that remove friction at the deal stage.
4. **Be the source the category cites.** Earn authority by being the original-data source other publications quote.
5. **Earn a direct line to marketers.** Build owned audiences (founder posts, REDO Academy) where REDO is part of the marketer's daily routine.
6. **Win the in-person conversation.** Trade shows and founder dinners convert higher than booths or DMs.
